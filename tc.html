📋 First Time Setup: Get Telegram API Credentials

1
Visit Telegram Developer Platform

Go to https://my.telegram.org and log in with your Telegram account (phone verification code required).

2
Create an Application

After logging in, click "API development tools", fill in an app name (e.g. "TelegramCloud") and a short name, then submit to get your api_id and api_hash.

3
Fill in the Software

Open TelegramCloud, enter the api_id, api_hash and your phone number in the login window, complete verification to start using.
